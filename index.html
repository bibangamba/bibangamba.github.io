how it works

1. Flight Information

the person traveling uploads their trip's information i.e. airline, date and time of flight, how much space they have available in kilograms, and how much they want for each kg

2. Package Information

the package owner browses through listed trips and requests to book space on a trip by submitting information about the package e.g. a list of the contents in package, the contact person (receiver or sender), package weight, type of space they'd like to book i.e. carry-on or checked-in

3. Confirmation

when the courier approves a package, the package owner gets a notification asking them or their contact person to bring the package over to the package office for checking. Once the package is checked in their presence and verified to not contain drugs or other contraband, the package owner pays the service fee in full. the courier then gets a notification asking them to pick up the package before their due flight.

4. Delivery

when the courier lands, they drop the package off at the country's package office where it's checked (to verify contents integrity) in the presence of the courier. once all the contents are accounted for, the courier receives payment for their service either in cash or via a bank deposit. the package owner or the contact person is then notified within the app to pick up their package
